ABOUT PHOTOGRAPHY

“Taking an image, freezing a moment, reveals how rich reality truly is.” We are making photographs to understand what our lives mean to us. I love to capture every thing in my life. It can be just a peace of red velvet cake when hungry, rainbow after rainstorm, or laughs and hugs at airport. I treasure every non-reproduceable moment of life. When in deepest sorrow, pictures with story can always bring consolation.

ABOUT MUSIC

Music has always been a huge part of my life from a very young age and till now it still remains it's powerful charm. Music makes me feel connected.

My passion towards music is probably because I have been playing the piano for over 17 years! (...I am old). Piano was the first instrument I played, and ever since than, I started playing the guitar, ukulele, drum set, as well as cucurbit flute(Hulusi), which is a free reed wind instrument from China. And I love to sing too!

When I don't feel like studying or doing work, I will usually sit on my bed playing the guitar, singing and recording music. I make Youtube video, hopefully my talent could be noticed by more people (well, if that's called "talent" haha). My audio works are located on Changba (a Chinese Karaoke app).
